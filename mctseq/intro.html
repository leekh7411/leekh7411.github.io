Monte-Carlo Tree Search(MCTS)-sequences

In silico selection of RNA aptamers for a target protein based on discriminative classifiers and the Monte-Carlo tree search




How to use this service?

Please confirm the right form to generate candidate aptamer sequences that have high binding affinity about the target protein sequence. After finish the task, we will send alarm email and check your results on the 'Results' page.



Task ID / Email

Please write your e-mail and unique task ID on the form. After the prediction job finished, we will send a notification through the e-mail and you can check the predictions in our result page.



Aptamer length / Iterations / Target protein

You can predict with specific length aptamer sequences (Aptammer length). The iteration is the number of loop in MCTS-seq sampling phase. Most important thing is a target protein sequence. Please write the amino-acid sequence of target protein in the form. MCTS-seq calculates a binding affinity score between sampled sequence and the protein. The results are used for better sample extraction.
